Hideo Kojima

The man with outstanding game design ideas

Here is the chronology of the creation of games as a designer Hideo Kojima:

  • 1986 - Penguin Adventure
  • 1987 - Metal Gear
  • 1988 - Snatcher
  • 1990 - Metal Gear 2: Solid Snake
  • 1994 - Policenauts
  • 1998 - Metal Gear Solid
  • 2001 - Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty
  • 2002 - Boktai: The Sun is in Your Hand
  • 2003 - Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty
  • 2004 - Metal Gear Solid: The Twin Snakes and Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater
  • 2004 - Metal Gear Solid 3: Snake Eater
  • 2008 - Super Smash Bros. Brawl (Designed Shadow Moses Island stage)
  • 2008 - Metal Gear Solid 4: Guns of the Patriots
  • 2008 - Metal Gear Online
  • 2010 - Metal Gear Solid: Peace Walker
  • 2014 - Metal Gear Solid V: Ground Zeroes
  • 2014 - P.T.
  • 2015 - Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain
  • 2019 - Death Stranding

"If Kojima continues to work so hard, he will shorten his life."
-- Mamoru Oshii
